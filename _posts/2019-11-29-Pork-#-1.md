# Ingredients
* 1 pound of pork ribs, cut into individual rib sections
* Water
* A pan with a lid, sieve, and stove!!
* A heat-proof dish!!
* Steaming rack
* White pepper
* 1 tbsp cooking wine
* 2 tsp sugar
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp sesame oil
* 3 slices of ginger
* 2 tsp cornstarch

# Directions
1. Wash the ribs

2. Mix everything edible in the dish, let it sit for a while.

3. Steam on medium-high or high heat for 10-15 minutes.
